Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MindCare

How accurate are the mental health assessments?
Our assessments are based on clinically validated screening tools and have been tested extensively. While they provide valuable insights, they are not a substitute for professional diagnosis. We always recommend consulting with a mental health professional for comprehensive evaluation.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data. All information is stored securely and anonymized for research purposes. We never share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent.
Can I use MindCare if I'm already seeing a therapist?
Yes! MindCare is designed to complement professional mental health care. Many users find that tracking their progress between sessions helps them have more productive conversations with their therapists.
How often should I take assessments?
We recommend taking assessments every 2-4 weeks to track your progress effectively. However, you can take them more frequently if you're experiencing significant changes or want to monitor specific situations.
What should I do if I get a high-risk result?
If you receive a high-risk result, we provide immediate resources including crisis helplines and guidance on seeking professional help. Remember, these results are indicators, not diagnoses, and reaching out to a mental health professional is the best next step.
