If You're in Immediate Danger

  1. Call emergency services (112) immediately if you or someone else is in life-threatening danger.
  2. Go to your nearest emergency room or hospital.
  3. Call a crisis helpline: KIRAN (1800-599-0019) or Vandrevala Foundation (9999-666-555).
  4. Don't stay alone – reach out to a trusted friend, family member, or neighbor.
  5. Remove access to means of self-harm and stay in a safe environment.

Remember: Crisis situations are temporary. With proper support and treatment, things can and do get better.
